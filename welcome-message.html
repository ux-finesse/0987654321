HOME / ABOUT / WELCOME MESSAGE

I am so excited to welcome you to Eliud International Schools. Since 2010, our school has worked tirelessly to focus on the growth of our students. Our ultimate goal is for every student who is entrusted to Eliud to develop into a lifelong learner and be outstanding in all aspects both in personal life and carreer. Furthermore, we work to encourage each student to commit to excellence through ground-breaking innovations and tried-and-true methods. The personal excellence that our staff exemplifies then ignites student achievement and growth.

As a crucial component of our efforts, we value parental input. We are confident that, with some help, our students can confidently plot a course toward meaning and achievement from that starting point.

Parents, extended family members, graduates, present students, and a host of others can attest that the mission of Eliud International School has played a significant role in the accomplishment of many goals and dreams. By God's grace, these assertions will hold true as we continue to raise up brave leaders and world changers.

Please accept my warm welcome to the Eliud International Schoool community. We created this website in an effort to convey the character and spirit of our school. To better understand and appreciate the power of our community and the significance of our mission, however, I strongly advise you to visit our campus. I'm looking forward to meeting you when you come to town. If you would like more information about Eliud, kindly get in touch with us. I would be delighted to meet you and your kid in person to talk more about the adventures of Eliud international School.

Warmest regards,

Mrs. Heather Pencil

Headmistress of School
