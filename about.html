“All different All equal”

WORDS THAT IMPLY ACTION AND ENDLESS PURSUING — A GLOBAL GOAL THAT IS NEVER COMPLETELY ATTAINTED AND A DREAM THAT IS NEVER COMPLETELY REALIZED. ALL DIFFERENT, ALL EQUAL! A MOTTO THAT ALLOWS US TO SEE EVERYONE EQUALLY.

At Eliud International School we offer a value based curriculum which incorporates aspects of the best curriculums in the world. It is tailored every term to meet the particular needs of the children. In addition to curriculum based subjects, we also offer Nigerian languages, Latin American dance eg salsa, cha cha, native dance, Etiquette & Elocution, Philosophy.

We believe that every child is unique and have the potential to be outstanding if properly nurtured, to achieve this we profile every child and work on thier strong points, we also implement all possible ways to bring out the best by applying different teaching techniques . It is an environment where they are allowed to make mistakes to learn. We try to make learning fun by using various resources, for example, learning through play. Our teachers are highly trained by overseas consultants which enables them to impart the highest level of knowledge to the students. At Eliud, sucessis is infectious.
